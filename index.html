About Me

My name is Mindy Pierre, and I am a graduate of Michigan Technological University with a Bachelor of Science in Scientific and Technical Communication. I am passionate about clear communication, technical writing, and helping users understand complex information in simple and useful ways.

I have experience working with technical documentation and user guides, particularly through my role as a Technical Writer at the Center for Technology and Training at Michigan Technological University. In that position I helped write and update documentation for the Roadsoft Software Suite, collaborated with developers and project managers, and supported users by clarifying workflows and solving documentation issues.

I enjoy working in environments where communication, organization, and problem-solving are important. My goal is to contribute to teams that focus on improving user experiences, creating effective documentation, and helping customers succeed with technology.

In addition to my professional work, I have also been involved in writing and campus organizations. I worked as a Staff Writer for The Lode newspaper and participated in student organizations including the Society for Intellectual Sisters, the Black Students Association, and the National Society of Black Engineers.
